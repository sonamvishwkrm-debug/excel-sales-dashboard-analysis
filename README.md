# excel-sales-dashboard-analysis
Sales data analysis using Excel with pivot tables and dashboard

📊 Sales Data Analysis – Key Insights
1️⃣ Business Profitability

The dashboard shows that the business is overall profitable, with total revenue significantly higher than total cost, resulting in a healthy profit margin.

However, profit performance varies by product category and region, indicating opportunities for optimization.

2️⃣ Product Category Performance

From Profit by Product & Country:

Services show the highest loss compared to other categories.

Hardware and Software perform relatively better but still show negative values in some regions.

Insight

The Services category may require cost optimization or pricing adjustments to improve profitability.

3️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend

The Monthly Revenue & MoM Growth chart shows fluctuating revenue across months.

Key observations:

Revenue peaks during certain months (around mid-year).

Some months show negative MoM growth, indicating temporary declines in sales.

Insight

Sales appear to follow seasonal demand patterns, suggesting opportunities for targeted marketing during low-performing months.

4️⃣ Regional Revenue Performance

From Revenue by Region:

North region generates the highest revenue compared to others.

Insight

The North region represents a high-performing market, which could be prioritized for expansion, marketing investment, and sales efforts.

5️⃣ Customer Type Profitability

From Profit by Customer Type:

New customers generate higher profit than existing customers.

Insight

Customer acquisition strategies are working effectively, but the company should focus on improving customer retention to increase long-term value.

6️⃣ Product Performance

From Top & Bottom Performing Products:

Revenue ranking:

Product B – Highest revenue

Product C – Second highest

Product D – Moderate performance

Product A – Lowest revenue

Insight

Product B is the primary revenue driver and should be prioritized in marketing and sales strategies.

7️⃣ City-Level Product Sales

From the London product analysis:

Product B generates the highest revenue

Product C performs the lowest

Insight

Customer demand differs by city and product, indicating that location-specific sales strategies may improve performance.

8️⃣ Profit vs Loss by Product

From Profit/Loss by Product:

Product B shows the highest profit

Product D generates the lowest profit

Insight

Product B is both a top revenue and profit contributor, making it a strategic product for business growth.

The company operates across multiple international markets, which diversifies revenue sources but may also introduce region-specific operational challenges.
